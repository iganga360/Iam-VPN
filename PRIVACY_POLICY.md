# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 31, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Iam-VPN ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

- **IP Addresses**: Your IP address is logged for abuse detection and security
- **Timestamps**: Date and time of connections
- **Requested URLs**: URLs accessed through the proxy (for abuse monitoring)
- **Connection Metadata**: Connection duration, data transferred (aggregate only)
- **Browser Information**: User agent strings

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Traffic Content**: We do not inspect or log the content of your browsing
- **DNS Queries**: We do not log individual DNS requests (when using our DNS)
- **Personal Information**: We do not require or store names, emails, or payment info (for free tier)

### 2.3 Optional Information

- **Authentication**: If using authentication features, we store:
  - User ID (randomly generated)
  - Authentication tokens (hashed)
  - Session information

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

### 3.1 Security and Abuse Prevention
- Detecting and preventing malicious activities
- Identifying DDoS attacks or network abuse
- Preventing spam and automated abuse
- Protecting the Service and other users

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyzing usage patterns (aggregated)
- Identifying performance issues
- Improving service reliability

### 3.3 Legal Compliance
- Responding to legal requests
- Complying with law enforcement when legally required
- Enforcing our Terms of Service

## 4. Data Retention

- **Connection Logs**: Retained for 7-30 days for abuse detection
- **Abuse Logs**: Retained for 90 days or as required by law
- **Authentication Tokens**: Expire after 1 hour
- **Aggregate Statistics**: May be retained indefinitely

## 5. Information Sharing

### 5.1 We DO Share:
- With law enforcement when legally required (subpoena, court order)
- With security researchers (anonymized, aggregate data only)
- When necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity

### 5.2 We DO NOT Share:
- With advertisers or marketers
- With data brokers
- For commercial purposes
- With third parties for their own use

## 6. Third-Party Services

The Service may route traffic through third-party networks. We are not responsible for:
- Third-party privacy practices
- Content or services accessed through the proxy
- External website policies

## 7. Security Measures

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication (JWT)
- Rate limiting and abuse detection
- Regular security audits
- Firewall protection

**However**, no system is 100% secure. Use the Service at your own risk.

## 8. DNS Leak Protection

When using our recommended configuration:
- DNS queries are routed through encrypted tunnels
- We recommend using 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare) or 1.0.0.1 for DNS
- Client-side DNS leak tests recommended
